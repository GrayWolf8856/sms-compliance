Privacy Policy — SMS Notifications

Effective date: May 31, 2026 · Operator: Kyle Cosper (Kyle Tech Corner Home Lab), USA

This Privacy Policy describes how Kyle Cosper ("we", "us") handles information in connection with the SMS notification service operated by the Kyle Tech Corner home lab.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. We do not sell, rent, or share mobile phone numbers or SMS consent data with any third party. All categories of this information are excluded from any third-party sharing.

Information we collect

The only information collected is the mobile phone number that the operator voluntarily enters into the self-hosted home lab application in order to receive automated notification text messages.

How we use information

The mobile phone number is used solely to deliver automated SMS notifications (system status alerts, monitoring notifications, environmental/weather monitoring, and backup reports). It is not used for marketing, advertising, or any other purpose.

SMS consent & sharing

SMS consent is obtained directly through an in-app consent checkbox and is never shared with anyone. Mobile opt-in data and consent are not shared with third parties for any purpose.

Message frequency & rates

Message frequency varies based on system events. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

Opt-out

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance.

Data retention & security

The phone number is stored only within the operator's private self-hosted configuration and is removed upon opt-out. No data is transmitted to external parties.
