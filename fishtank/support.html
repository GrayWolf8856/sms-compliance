Fishtank Tracker

Support

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Fishtank Tracker is an offline-first iPhone app for tracking aquarium water quality — log test-strip readings, monitor trends, and keep a separate history for each of your tanks. Everything is stored privately on your device.

Getting started

  • Add a tank with its volume.
  • Record a water test by entering values manually or scanning a test strip with your camera.
  • Review charts and color-coded status indicators to spot trends over time.

Frequently asked

Do I need an internet connection? No. The app works fully offline.

Where is my data stored? On your device only. See the Privacy Policy.

The strip scan looks off — what can I do? Make sure the strip is well lit and aligned inside the on-screen guide. You can also enter any reading manually at any time.
