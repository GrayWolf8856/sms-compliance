Fishtank Tracker

An offline-first iPhone app for tracking aquarium water quality. Log test-strip readings, watch trends, and keep a history for every tank — all stored privately on your device.

Support Privacy Policy

What it does

Stay on top of water quality and keep your fish thriving — whether you keep one tank or several.

🧪

Log every parameter

Track ammonia, nitrite, nitrate, pH, chlorine, hardness, and alkalinity over time.

📷

Scan or enter by hand

Read a test strip with your camera, or type values in manually — your choice.

📈

Trends & status

Color-coded status and charts show whether a parameter is safe and where it's heading.

🔒

Private & offline

Works 100% offline. No account, no ads, no tracking. Your data stays on your device.
