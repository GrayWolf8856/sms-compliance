About Kyle Tech Corner

Kyle Tech Corner is a personal tech channel about home labs, self-hosting, networking, and hands-on DIY builds — run by Kyle, a lifelong tinkerer.

What it's about

It's one person's playground for learning by building: small always-on servers running at home, automation, monitoring and alerting projects, networking experiments, and weekend hardware hacks — documented and shared on the @KyleTechCorner YouTube channel.

The home lab

At the center is a self-hosted home lab — a small set of machines that host services, automate everyday tasks, and run real monitoring and alerting. There's a full breakdown of how it's wired together on the Homelab page.

Follow along

New builds and walkthroughs land on YouTube — @KyleTechCorner. That's the best place to keep up with the projects.
